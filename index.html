Speech Samples

(a) parallel-VC : A sequence-to-sequence voice conversion system with parallel speech data between the foreign accented and native speakers.
(b) BNF-FAC : This is a FAC system that takes PPG as input and generates acoustic features [1].
(c) BNF-PC-FAC: This system is an extension from system (b) with a pronuciation correction model [2].
(d) TTS-FAC : Our proposed FAC system with TTS pretraining.

Foreign Accented Speech: The natural speech from a non-native target speaker, we hope to remove the foreign accents in the speech and preserve the speaker identity.
Native Speech: The natural speech from a native English speaker. It is used as a reference.
